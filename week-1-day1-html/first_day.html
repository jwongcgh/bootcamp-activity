How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Slice bread, two should be enough for one sandwich
  2. Optional: Spread butter on one side of sliced bread
  3. Place a leaf of lettuce on top of one of the bread slices, buttered side up
  4. Place one or more slices of your favorite hams
  5. Add a slice of yummy cheese on top
  6. Optional: spread some thick sauce such as tartar sauce
  7. Add slices of pickles, distribute them evenly around
  8. Add sliced olives, distribute them evenly around
  9. Add a slice of tomato, better if freshly cut
  10. Another alice of cheese, makes it yummier
  11. One more slice of lettuce
  12. The other piece of bread goes on top, buttered side down
  13. Enjoy with a cold beer
